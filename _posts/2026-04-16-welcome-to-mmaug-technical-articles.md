We're excited to launch the **MMAUG Technical Articles** platform — a community-driven space where members of the Malta Microsoft AI User Group can publish and share technical knowledge.

## Why Technical Articles?

Our community thrives on knowledge sharing. While our meetups and YouTube recordings capture live sessions, written articles offer something different:

- **Depth** — articles let authors dive deep into a topic with code samples, diagrams, and step-by-step walkthroughs.
- **Searchability** — written content is easy to search, reference, and share.
- **Accessibility** — articles are available anytime, anywhere, at your own pace.
- **Portfolio** — contributing an article builds your professional profile and showcases your expertise.

## What Can You Write About?

We welcome articles on any topic within the MMAUG scope:

- **Microsoft AI** — Azure OpenAI, AI Studio, Cognitive Services, ML workflows
- **Azure Cloud** — architecture patterns, infrastructure, networking, security
- **DevOps** — CI/CD pipelines, GitHub Actions, infrastructure as code
- **Open Source** — projects and tools in the Microsoft ecosystem
- **Tutorials** — hands-on guides for developers and engineers
- **Community insights** — lessons learned, event recaps, career advice in tech

## How It Works

Our articles platform is built on **GitHub Pages** with **Jekyll**, and contributions follow a transparent, open-source workflow:

1. **Fork** the [community repository](https://github.com/maltamsaiusergroup/maltamsaiusergroup)
2. **Write** your article in Markdown inside the `_posts/` folder
3. **Submit** a Pull Request for review
4. **Publish** — once approved and merged, your article goes live automatically

No special tools needed — just Markdown and a GitHub account.
